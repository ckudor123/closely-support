# Closely Support

Closely - The Bonding Game is made for simple, thoughtful conversations with couples, friends, family, and groups.

## Contact

For support, feedback, privacy questions, or app issues, email:

hello.closely.app@gmail.com

## Helpful Details to Include

When you write to us, it helps to include:

- what device you are using
- which app version you are using
- what you were trying to do
- what happened instead
- screenshots, if they make the issue easier to understand

## Privacy

Closely does not require an account. Saved question lists, players, settings, and game progress stay on your device unless you choose to contact us by email.

For more detail, publish `docs/privacy-policy.md` as the public privacy policy before App Store submission.
