# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Closely - The Bonding Game is designed to feel private, simple, and safe to use with the people around you. This policy explains what stays on your device, what may be sent when you choose to contact us, and how you can control your local data.

## Information Stored on Your Device

Closely stores the following information locally on your device so the app can work smoothly:

- saved question lists
- current game progress
- app language preference
- whether you have already seen the first-time introduction
- local settings, such as haptics

This information is not automatically sent to Closely servers.

## Information We Do Not Collect Automatically

Closely does not require an account.

Closely does not automatically collect or send your saved question lists, players, or game progress to our servers.

Closely does not track you across apps or websites.

Closely does not use third-party advertising.

## Feedback and Support

If you choose to send feedback or contact support from inside the app, Closely opens your email app with a message addressed to the Closely support address. We use the email and message you send only to read your message, respond when needed, and improve the app.

## Deleting Local Data

You can delete local app data from Settings inside Closely. This removes saved lists and local progress from the device. Deleted local data cannot be recovered.

## Children

Closely is not designed for children and is not intended for the App Store Kids category.

## Changes

We may update this policy when Closely changes. If our data practices change, we will update this policy and the app privacy information before release.
